Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

IPO Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

1. Information We Collect

IPO Tracker does not collect any personal information from users. The app displays publicly available IPO data sourced from our database. We do not require users to create accounts or provide any personal details.

2. Data Storage

All IPO data displayed in the app is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database. No personal user data is stored on our servers.

3. Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to fetch IPO data from our Firebase database. We do not track your browsing activity or collect any usage data.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

Google Firebase — Used to store and retrieve IPO data. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
